The Story

Where Japanese papercraft meets dark whimsical fantasy. A design system born from the intersection of tradition and imagination.

☘ Chapter 1: The Fiber

From the kozo tree's inner bark, generations of craftsmen have extracted the essence of washi — paper that breathes, that ages with grace, that carries the soul of the maker. We begin with this fiber, this foundation of all that follows.

★ Chapter 2: The Cut

Kirigami — the art of cutting paper to reveal what lies beneath. Each incision is a decision, each fold a transformation. Our components peel back layers to expose the patterns hidden within.

🕸 Chapter 3: The Dream

In the space between waking and sleep, dragonflies hover above enchanted waters. Fantasy merges with craft. The Cheshire grin appears in swirling ribbons of text. Our interface comes alive with motion and mystery.

✿ Chapter 4: The Craft

Twenty components. Five sacred dyes. One living interface. Kozo Dreams is not merely a design system — it is an atmosphere, a responsive world of paper and pixel that adapts, breathes, and responds to every interaction.
