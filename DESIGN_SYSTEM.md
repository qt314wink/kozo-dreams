### Fused Aesthetic: "Kozo Dreams"

A design language where traditional Japanese papercraft meets dark whimsical fantasy. The interface feels like opening a mysterious handcrafted book — each page is textured washi paper, but the illustrations move, the text writhes like ribbons, and fantasy creatures peek through kirigami-cut windows.

**Atmospheric Keywords:**
- Fibrous, Deckled, Glowing, Floating, Whispering, Halftone, Inky, Translucent, Organically-mechanical, Dreamlike
